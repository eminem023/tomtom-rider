TomTom Rider Map Update

TomTom Rider devices are designed specifically for motorcycle adventures. Regular map updates help keep your GPS routes accurate and safe. This guide provides all the steps needed to update maps on your TomTom Rider GPS device.

Benefits of Map Updates

  • Access to the latest roads, routes, and speed limits
  • Improved scenic ride planning and off-route recalculations
  • Updated POIs including gas stations and rest stops
  • Enhanced GPS accuracy and performance

Requirements for Updating

  • Your TomTom Rider GPS (e.g., 400, 500, 550)
  • Stable Wi-Fi connection or USB cable and computer
  • TomTom account credentials
  • MyDrive Connect (for USB updates)

How to Update Your Rider Maps

Option 1: Update via Wi-Fi

Turn on Wi-Fi on your TomTom Rider. Connect to your network and go to Settings > Updates & New Items. If updates are available, follow the prompts to install them directly on the device.

Option 2: Update via MyDrive Connect

  • Download and install MyDrive Connect on your PC or Mac
  • Connect the Rider device via USB
  • Open the software, log in, and wait for available updates
  • Select and install the map updates shown

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Wi-Fi not working: Restart device or try another network
  • Update stalls: Close and reopen MyDrive Connect or restart your PC
  • Storage full: Remove old maps or add a microSD card
